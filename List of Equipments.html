Dumbbell Set

The dumbbell set that is described includes a variety of weights, ranging from 5lbs to 50lbs, and comes with a rack to make storing the dumbbells easy. This set is ideal for strength training exercises and can be used to target specific muscle groups. Dumbbell exercises are versatile and can be used to work out various muscle groups such as biceps, triceps, chest, shoulders, back, and legs. Additionally, dumbbell exercises can improve overall strength, muscle endurance, and even help with weight loss.

Treadmill

Our treadmill is designed to help you improve your cardiovascular health and burn calories. It features adjustable speed and incline settings, as well as heart rate monitoring, which can help you track your progress and stay within your target heart rate zone. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced runner, our treadmill can provide a challenging workout that can help you reach your fitness goals. To use the treadmill, start with a warm-up at a slower speed and gradually increase the speed and incline as you feel comfortable.

Learn More

Stationary Bicycle

To use our stationary bicycle, start by adjusting the seat to a comfortable height. Then, select a resistance level that challenges you but allows you to maintain a consistent pedaling motion. Begin pedaling at a moderate pace and gradually increase your speed and resistance as your endurance improves. Use the digital display to track your progress and aim for at least 30 minutes of cycling per session, gradually increasing the duration and intensity over time. Remember to stay hydrated and listen to your body to avoid injury.

Learn More

Barbell Set

The amount of weight to lift during each repetition and the number of sets and repetitions to perform with our barbell set depends on your fitness goals and current fitness level. As a general guideline, for building strength and muscle mass, you can aim for 3-4 sets of 8-12 repetitions with a weight that challenges you but still allows you to maintain proper form. Always start with a warm-up set with lighter weights to prevent injury and gradually increase the weight as you become more comfortable with the exercise.

Rowing Machine

Our rowing machine is designed with adjustable resistance levels and a comfortable seat to ensure a great workout experience. The digital display helps to track distance, time, and calories burned during exercise. This full-body workout machine is ideal for improving cardiovascular health, building endurance, and strengthening back, arms, and legs. Its low-impact nature also makes it perfect for people of all fitness levels.
